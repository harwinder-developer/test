1.1.0

April 4, 2016
  • NEW: Three new emails! Send your campaign creators a notification when they receive a donation, an email after they submit their campaign and an email a few days before their campaign ends (you can choose how many days ahead of time the email should go out). Go to Charitable > Emails to enable and configure your new email options!
  • NEW: Auto-approve campaigns when they are submitted. No more code snippets required! Just enable the feature and you're done.
  • NEW: [charitable_creator_donations] shortcode, which displays a list of a campaign creator's received donations when they are logged in.
  • NEW: Choose the payout method you use for campaign creators submitting personal campaigns. By default, PayPal is the only option, but stay tuned for a Stripe Connect extension that will be included in the Charitable Pro package.
  • Makes the Category a non-required field by default, and fixes a bug that made it impossible to submit campaigns when no campaign categories have been created.
  • Fixes a bug that prevented the new campaign email from getting sent when a campaign is submitted.
  • Fixes a bug that stopped mo/po translations from being used correctly.
  • Fixes a bug in the [charitable_my_campaigns] shortcode that prevented the global $post variable from being reset properly after the shortcode.
  • Deprecated certain hooks:
    • charitable_ambassadors_admin_start -- Use Charitable_Ambassadors_Admin::get_instance() instead.
